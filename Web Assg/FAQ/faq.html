FAQ

We at Nexon My Furniture are a leading furniture manufacturer in Kuala Lumpur with years of providing customized furniture for homes, hotels, offices, government buildings and more.

What materials are your furniture pieces made of ?

Our furniture pieces are made of high-quality materials such as solid wood, metal, glass, and various engineered materials like MDF, plywood, and particleboard. Each product page includes a detailed list of materials used in the construction of that particular piece.

What is your return policy ?

We have a 30-day return policy for all non-custom items. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Custom items, such as made-to-order furniture pieces, cannot be returned unless there is a defect in the product.

Can I customize my furniture ?

Some of our furniture pieces can be customized with different finishes, colors, or sizes. Customization options will vary depending on the product, and additional fees may apply.

What if my furniture arrives damaged ?

If your furniture arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will work with you to resolve the issue and arrange for a replacement or refund if necessary.
